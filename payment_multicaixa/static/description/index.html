Register your self with EMIS

Register your self with EMIS entity to provide the you with a Entity Reference Number and API Key, and to allow your reference to be recognized in Multicaixa Network.

Set your settings the module

Define your Entity Reference and API Key

Set your Entity reference, API Key and the Expiry Date that your payment reference is available.

Entity reference and API Keys is provided by EMIS. The Expiry Date will allow to set the maximum time your references will be available for payment. This will automatically add the number of days to the date order creation.

Your Online Store Integrated With Multicaixa

No credit card? Pay from Multicaixa or Internet Banking

From your online store, your clients able to select Multicaixa as payment method and pay from internet banking or by going to nearest Multicaixa.

When the payment is performed, your sale will automatically be notified and confirmed. Simple and easy.

How to pay from Multicaixa?

Go to the nearest Multicaixa ATM

After your card secret PIN inserted, select Payment Services, insert the Entity reference, your sale order reference, confirm and pay!

Insert the sale or invoice reference, and a window will be shown with all amount in payment. Confirm the amount you want to pay.

From payment window, confirm the amount and do Pay Invoice. Depending of traffic, when you return to online shop your order is already confirmed.

How to pay from internet Banking?

Well, get a Angolan bank account with internet banking, use the option for payment services and pay. Your order will be confirmed automatically.
